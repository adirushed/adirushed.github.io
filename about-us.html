About Sky Green Waste

SKY Green Waste was incorporated to focus on reducing the storage of used and discarded electronic equipment, which are mostly land filed or recycled by unorganised sector in unhygienic way leading to severe health hazards to poor workforce. SKY Green Waste has established itself as India’s leading provider of IT Asset Disposition services. SKY Green Waste serves India’s leading IT, IT-enabled, telecom, Pharmaceuticals, manufacturing and Hospitality sectors companies to manage their electronic waste in an environmental friendly manner while also generating revenues for the clients, complying norms set by the Pollution Control Board for the environmental safety.

SKY Green Waste specializes in end-of-life electronics recycling, the process by which obsolete electronic devices are dismantled and separated into clean, reusable commodities. SKY Green Waste is able to efficiently and effectively process large volumes of end-of-life electronics (also known as e-waste) utilizing automated recycling equipment and cutting-edge technology.
